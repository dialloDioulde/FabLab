Welcome to our Website, Thank you for visiting.

This Website is set up to facilitate the Management of Material Loans to the Fablab FabMSTIC from Grenoble Alpes University.

In other words, the website allows you to keep track of the equipment or materials borrowed by a Borrower and therefore it guarantees the proper use of the Materials by all the beneficiaries.

On this Website you can find all of the Materials that can be loaned by the Fablab FabMSTIC as well as the conditions to be fulfilled in order to Borrow a Material.

The Charter that you must know before claiming to request a Loan is available on this site in the CHARTER tab.

The Fablab FabMSTIC is a workshop space of the Grenoble IT Laboratory.

The idea of ​​creating this website is entirely the work of Mr. Germain LEMASSON.

This Website was produced by DIALLO Mamadou and MIHALI Kristi both students as part of the Ter Project of their Master 1 IT under the supervision of Mr. Germain LEMASSON.
