USER MANUAL

In this page you can find the procedure allowing to CREATE, DISPLAY the list of Loans Materials, EDIT and DELETE a loan material.

All the operations that can be carried out on loans materials are accessible to all users with an account.

1. CREATE A LOAN MATERIAL :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - To Create a Loan Material click on the LOGO of the website visible in the navigation bar.

3 - All Materials available for a Loan will be displayed.

4 - Click on the ADD TO LOAN button of the Material to be loaned to create the loan basket.

5 - Click on the LOAN button visible in the navigation bar to display the Materials basket.

6 - To add the quantity of a Material in the current basket click on the + button present in the QUANTITY column and corresponding to the Material concerned.

7 - To reduce the quantity of Material in the current basket click on the - button present in the QUANTITY column and corresponding to the Material concerned.

8 - To delete a Material from the basket of the current Loan, click on the icon symbolizing the delete button located in the COMMANDS column and corresponding to the Material concerned.

9 - Once the basket of Materials is displayed if you want to add a new Material to the current loan click on the CONTINUE ADDING button.

10 - Finally to create the loan of Materials it is necessary to associate a Borrower with the Loan.

11 - Click on the PROCEED TO CHECKOUT button to add the BORROWER and enter an EXPECTED RETURN DATE for the Borrowed Materials.

12 - Click on the SAVE LOAN button to complete the Loan Creation.
