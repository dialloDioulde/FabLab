USER MANUAL

In this page you can find the procedure allowing to CREATE, DISPLAY the list of Borrowers, MODIFY and DELETE a borrower.

All the operations that can be carried out on borrowers are accessible to all users with an account.

1. CREATE A BORROWER :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - Click on the DASHBOARD tab located in the navigation bar.

3 - You will be redirected to the page containing the list of all the Loans already made on the website.

4 - Move the mouse cursor completely to the left and click on the LOANER tab to access the list of all registered borrowers.

5 - You also have access to the functionalities allowing to CREATE a Borrower, to EDIT the information of a Borrower and to DELETE a Borrower.

6 - To create a Borrower click on the ADD LOANER button, Fill in the fields : LAST NAME, FIRST NAME, EMAIL, ESTABLISHMENT and click on the ADD LOANER button to complete the creation of the new borrower.

2. SHOW LIST OF BORROWERS :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - Click on the DASHBOARD tab located in the navigation bar.

3 - You will be redirected to the page containing the list of all the Loans already made on the website.

4 - Move the mouse cursor completely to the left and click on the LOANER tab to access the list of all registered borrowers.

5 - You also have access to the functionalities allowing to CREATE a Borrower, to EDIT the information of a Borrower and to DELETE a Borrower.

3. EDIT A BORROWER :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - Click on the DASHBOARD tab located in the navigation bar.

3 - You will be redirected to the page containing the list of all the Loans already made on the website.

4 - Move the mouse cursor completely to the left and click on the LOANER tab to access the list of all registered borrowers.

5 - You also have access to the functionalities allowing to CREATE a Borrower, to EDIT the information of a Borrower and to DELETE a Borrower.

6 - Click on the EDIT button the borrower to edit to display its edit form with this information previously entered.

7 - Edit the field (s) concerned by the edition and click on the Save Changes button to save your modifications.

4. DELETE A BORROWER :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - Click on the DASHBOARD tab located in the navigation bar.

3 - You will be redirected to the page containing the list of all the Loans already made on the website.

4 - Move the mouse cursor completely to the left and click on the LOANER tab to access the list of all registered borrowers.

5 - You also have access to the functionalities allowing to CREATE a Borrower, to EDIT the information of a Borrower and to DELETE a Borrower.

6 - Click the DELETE Borrower button to delete. 7 - You will be asked to confirm the deletion, click OK to complete the action or click CANCEL to cancel the deletion.
