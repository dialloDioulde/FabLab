USER MANUAL

In this page you can find the procedure allowing to CREATE, DISPLAY the list of Materials, EDIT and DELETE a material.

All the operations that can be carried out on materials are accessible to all users with an account.

1. CREATE A MATERIAL :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - Click on the DASHBOARD tab located in the navigation bar.

3 - You will be redirected to the page containing the list of all the Loans already made on the website.

4 - Move the mouse cursor completely to the left and click on the MATERIAL tab to access the list of all registered materials.

5 - You also have access to the functionalities allowing to CREATE a Material, to EDIT the information of a Material and to DELETE a Material.

6 - To create a Material click on the ADD MATERIAL button, Fill in the fields :
NAME, BARCODE, TYPE, MATERIAL PICTURE and click on the SUBMIT MATERIAL button to complete the creation of the new material.

7 - PLEASE NOTE :
A single TYPE can only be associated with one and only MATERIAL.
A generic TYPE can be associated with several MATERIALS.

2. SHOW LIST OF TMATERIALS :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - Click on the DASHBOARD tab located in the navigation bar.

3 - You will be redirected to the page containing the list of all the Loans already made on the website.

4 - Move the mouse cursor completely to the left and click on the MATERIAL tab to access the list of all registered materials.

5 - You also have access to the functionalities allowing to CREATE a Material, to EDIT the information of a Material and to DELETE a Material.

3. EDIT A MATERIAL :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - Click on the DASHBOARD tab located in the navigation bar.

3 - You will be redirected to the page containing the list of all the Loans already made on the website.

4 - Move the mouse cursor completely to the left and click on the MATERIAL tab to access the list of all registered materials.

5 - You also have access to the functionalities allowing to CREATE a Material, to EDIT the information of a Material and to DELETE a Material.

6 - Click on the EDIT button the material to edit to display its edit form with this information previously entered.

7 - Edit the field (s) concerned by the edition and click on the SUBMIT MATERIAL button to save your modifications.

4. DELETE A MATERIAL :

1 - To access this functionality, you must be logged in.

2 - Click on the DASHBOARD tab located in the navigation bar.

3 - You will be redirected to the page containing the list of all the Loans already made on the website.

4 - Move the mouse cursor completely to the left and click on the MATERIAL tab to access the list of all registered materials.

5 - You also have access to the functionalities allowing to CREATE a Material, to EDIT the information of a Material and to DELETE a Material.

6 - Click the DELETE material button to delete.

7 - You will be asked to confirm the deletion, click OK to complete the action or click CANCEL to cancel the deletion.
